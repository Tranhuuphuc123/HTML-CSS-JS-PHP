THE BAND

We are the one!

"The Harmonics" is a four-piece band comprising lead vocalist Sarah, guitarist Alex, bassist Mark, and drummer Lisa. Each member brings their own distinct musical background and influences, resulting in a sound that is both eclectic and harmonious. With a repertoire that spans various genres such as rock, pop, and alternative, "The Harmonics" effortlessly combines catchy melodies, heartfelt lyrics, and energetic rhythms. Their music resonates with listeners of all ages, appealing to both mainstream and indie music enthusiasts.
